The Future of Journalism

I could give y'all my whole backstory, the whole nine, but instead I want to take a chance to talk about the future of Journalism. As a young person, I see that the need for good journalism is more important than ever. We need new storytellers bringing in different perspectives, and we need innovative ways to tell these stories in the modern age. I have worked independently as a freelancer, but my passion for journalism comes from collaboration, and the ability to create stories with a team. Journalism today can reach more people than at any other moment in history, which requires responsible, socially conscious journalistic practices. It also requires gumption, a willingness to fail, and the desire to reimagine. This is the future of journalism, and I'm determined to be a part of it.
