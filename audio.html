Audio work

The Morning Mountain presented by the Colorado Springs Gazette, July 2022 - Nov. 2022

In 2022, Jackson Bailey and I pitched a news podcast to the Colorado Springs Gazette, and produced 14 episodes together over the span of 5 months. The idea was to originally highlight reporting work of the Gazette reporters working on a series of stories on the Colorado River, and the project blossomed into covering daily stories, national headlines, and interesting pieces that needed more in-depth coverage.
