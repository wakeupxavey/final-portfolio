Selects

A collection of my work from the past few years

At Oakland polls, voters have hope — and a bad case of the jitters

Election day reporting with fellow UC Berkeley Students in Oakland, CA. On-the-street interviews with Oaklanders at the polls, discussing their thoughts and feelings on this election. Organization: Oaklandside

What has become of the effort to bring a historically Black college to Oakland?

Investigation into the former mayor of Oakland plans to develop a project to bring an HBCU satellite campus to the city. Former Deputy Mayor of Oakland sheds light on the plan's impact, and how the mayor’s office is moving forward with the project. Organization: Oakland North

Websites

ZSITE

A personal website to accompany my master's thesis project, Z Sh!t, a youtube channel dedicated to telling stories about my generation, to my generation. The website is a hub for the channel, and includes links to the channel, as well as an interactive game and simple sticky note wall. Self-designed and self-coded.

Grocery Madness in the East Bay

This is a website my classmates and I created for a project documenting grocery prices in the Easy Bay of California. Across Oakland and Berkeley, we recorded and compared various common grocery items. The project took place amidst the "egg crisis" of 2025, and compiled our self-collected data into a few interactive features, graphs, and maps to create this community resource piece. Group designed and self-coded.

Ink Fever

A group project exploring the history and cultural impact of Tattoos. Features several interactive features including the ability to draw on the page, and select your own story path by selecting different tattoos on a svg body. Group designed and self-coded.
