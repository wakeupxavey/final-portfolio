Video work

The Allegro Ballroom

In a team, we explored a local dance hall that has existed for many years, in its new home in Albany, CA. This project was a school assignment. I took on the roles of editor, and assistant videographer.

Campo de Flores

At the University of Puget Sound, I worked on this project with my classmates, taking a look at immigration stories from our community, trying to understand what made people leave their homes to start a new life in the United States. I took on the roles of editor, interviewer and script-writer.
